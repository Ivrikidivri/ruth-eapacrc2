Our History

The East Asia & Pacific Regional Council of Cheshire Homes has a rich legacy of empowering people with disabilities across the region. This page shares our journey, key milestones, and the values that continue to inspire us today.

1992s

When some of the Council Members found that the A.G.M s were too costly, they propose that our Constitution be amended so that the Biennial General Meetings could be held instead.

The amendment was approved in 1992 and thenceforth Biennial General Meeting were held as follows:

1. 1 st B.G.M Penang, Malaysia 1992

2. 2 nd B.G.M Kunming, China 1994

3. 3rd B.G.M Jakarta, Indonesia 1996

4. 4th B.G.M Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia 1998

5. 5th B.G.M Wuhan, China 2000

6. 6th B.G.M Augi, Hyogo, Japan 2002

7. 7th B.G.M Bangkok, Thailand 2004

8. 8th B.G.M Singapore 2006

9. 9th B.G.M Hong Kong 2008.

Today
