The EAPacRC Secretariat

The East Asia & Pacific Regional Council (EAPacRC) of Cheshire Homes is a collaborative body of member organizations working together across our region.

We are driven by a shared vision: "A world where every person with a disability lives with dignity, inclusion, and independence".

EAPacRC exists to connect people and organizations, to learn from one another, and to act together.

Through collaboration, we share experiences, address challenges, and create solutions that open doors to equal opportunities and meaningful participation for every PwD.
